The Visionaries

Jules Rimet & The Birth of World Football

In 1928, Jules Rimet and his colleagues dared to dream of a tournament that would transcend borders and unify the world through sport. Their conviction laid the foundation for the most-watched event on the planet.

The "Coupe du Monde" was more than a trophy; it was a testament to the human spirit. Today, we continue that legacy with more teams and more passion than ever before.
