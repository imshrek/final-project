About World Cup 2026

The Journey Continues

The FIFA World Cup 2026 represents a milestone moment in football history. For the first time, the tournament will be hosted across three nations, bringing the beautiful game to new audiences and creating unprecedented opportunities for global celebration.

This tournament symbolizes unity through sport, where countries transcend boundaries to compete in the spirit of fair play and excellence. Nations from every continent will gather to showcase their talent, determination, and passion for football.
